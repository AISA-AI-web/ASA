ASA Categories
Every activity belongs to one of these four categories. Use this as a quick reference while browsing the directory.
🌿
Reflective & Wellness
Nurturing balance, mindfulness, and well-being (e.g. Sustainability Club, Quran Club, Peace Leaders).
📚
Academics & Enrichment
Extending learning beyond the classroom (e.g. STEM Club, Number Ninjas, Robotics, Study Halls).
⚽️
Athletics & Movement
Physical fitness and teamwork (e.g. PASS Football, Zumba, Learn To Swim, Basketball).
🎨
Arts & Creativity
Imagination and self-expression (e.g. Creative Writing, DIY Club, Ballet, Guitar).
