Registration Process

Follow the steps below to register your child for an ASA. Times differ slightly between Elementary and Secondary programs.

🏫 Elementary Process

01
End of School Day
School finishes at 14:50.
02
ASA Meet-Up
Students with an ASA meet their instructor on the Lower Basketball Court.
03
Attendance
Attendance completed on Mograsys by ASA Instructor.
04
ASA Timing
15:00–16:00 — Students will be in their ASAs!
05
Dismissal & Pick-Up
Dismissed at 16:00 from their ASA. Pick-up at Gate 4. Please be prompt!

🏫 Secondary Process

01
End of School Day
School finishes at 14:50.
02
ASA Meet-Up
Secondary students head to their assigned ASA location (HS Library, Homeroom, Greenhouse, Soccer Field, etc.)
03
Attendance
Attendance completed on Mograsys by ASA Instructor.
04
ASA Timing
15:00–16:00 — Students will be in their ASAs!
05
Dismissal & Pick-Up
Dismissed at 16:00 from their ASA. Pick-up at Gate 4. Please be prompt!
⚠️
Please note that all schedules are subject to change based on decisions made by the UAE Government, ADEK (Abu Dhabi Department of Education and Knowledge), and the School Administration.
