Welcome to AISA
After School Activities

At the American International School Abu Dhabi, learning does not end when the school day finishes — it expands, deepens, and comes alive through our After School Activities (ASA) program.

Our ASAs are a natural extension of our mission: Developing resilient learners and compassionate leaders for a dynamic world.

Each activity — whether athletic, creative, academic, or reflective — is intentionally designed to provide students with opportunities to explore their passions, challenge themselves, and grow as individuals within a supportive community.

📚

Categories of ASAs

Four categories cover the breadth of the ASA program. Every activity in the directory belongs to one of these.

🌿
Reflective & Wellness
Nurturing balance, mindfulness, and well-being through activities that support a healthy body and mind.
Examples: Sustainability Club, Greenhouse Care, Quran Club, Peace Leaders
📚
Academics & Enrichment
Extending learning beyond the classroom by building knowledge, curiosity, and critical thinking skills.
Examples: STEM Club, Number Ninjas, Robotics & Coding, IB English Study Hall
Athletics & Movement
Promoting physical fitness, teamwork, and resilience through active and engaging movement experiences.
Examples: PASS Football, Zumba, Learn To Swim, CUBS Sports
🎨
Arts & Creativity
Inspiring imagination and self-expression through artistic exploration, design, and creative thinking.
Examples: Creative Writing, DIY Club, Ballet, Guitar Lessons
Explore. Create. Grow.

We invite every student to take part in the ASA program — to discover new interests, build lasting relationships, and continue their journey as confident, capable, and caring AISA Lions.
