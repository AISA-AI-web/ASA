Welcome

Welcome to AISA
After School Activities

At the American International School Abu Dhabi, learning does not end when the school day finishes — it expands, deepens, and comes alive through our After School Activities (ASA) program.

Our ASAs are a natural extension of our mission: Developing resilient learners and compassionate leaders for a dynamic world.

Each activity — whether athletic, creative, academic, or reflective — is intentionally designed to provide students with opportunities to explore their passions, challenge themselves, and grow as individuals within a supportive community.

🌿
The AISA Lion Way
Be Responsible. Be Safe. Be Respectful. Be Kind.
📚
Learning Beyond the Classroom
Academic growth, creative expression, physical development & well-being.
🤝
A Community of Belonging
Students build friendships, develop empathy & strengthen their sense of belonging.
🌍
Growing Global Citizens
Collaboration, leadership & intercultural understanding prepare students for the world.

Types of ASAs

🌿
Reflective & Wellness
Nurturing balance, mindfulness, and well-being through activities that support a healthy body and mind.
Examples: Sustainability Club, Greenhouse Care, Quran Club, Peace Leaders
📚
Academics & Enrichment
Extending learning beyond the classroom by building knowledge, curiosity, and critical thinking skills.
Examples: STEM Club, Number Ninjas, Robotics & Coding, IB English Study Hall
⚽️
Athletics & Movement
Promoting physical fitness, teamwork, and resilience through active and engaging movement experiences.
Examples: PASS Football, Zumba, Learn To Swim, CUBS Sports
🎨
Arts & Creativity
Inspiring imagination and self-expression through artistic exploration, design, and creative thinking.
Examples: Creative Writing, DIY Club, Ballet, Guitar Lessons

ASA Process

🏫 Elementary Process

01
End of School Day
School finishes at 14:50.
02
ASA Meet-Up
Students with an ASA meet their instructor on the Lower Basketball Court.
03
Attendance
Attendance completed on Mograsys by ASA Instructor.
04
ASA Timing
15:00–16:00 — Students will be in their ASAs!
05
Dismissal & Pick-Up
Dismissed at 16:00 from their ASA. Pick-up at Gate 4. Please be prompt!

🏫 Secondary Process

01
End of School Day
School finishes at 14:50.
02
ASA Meet-Up
Secondary students head to their assigned ASA location (HS Library, Homeroom, Greenhouse, Soccer Field, etc.)
03
Attendance
Attendance completed on Mograsys by ASA Instructor.
04
ASA Timing
15:00–16:00 — Students will be in their ASAs!
05
Dismissal & Pick-Up
Dismissed at 16:00 from their ASA. Pick-up at Gate 4. Please be prompt!

❓ Frequently Asked Questions

How can my child join an activity?
Please scan the QR code provided or follow the link shared via email. You may select activities based on your child's grade-level offerings.
Are there fees involved when joining an activity?
Some activities require full payment upon registration. Certain activities may collect a partial fee to cover materials. A range of free activities is also available; however, registration is still required. Detailed instructions will be shared in the Welcome Letter from the activity coordinator.
How long does an activity run?
Most activities are scheduled to last for approximately 8 weeks. Any changes will be communicated in advance through email.
What should my child bring or prepare for each session?
Any specific requirements will be shared ahead of time by the activity coordinator via email to ensure students are well-prepared.
Is transportation provided for off-campus activities?
Transportation is available for selected off-campus activities (e.g., bowling, equestrian club, Cubs sports). Parents/guardians are kindly asked to collect their children from AISA campus after the activity.
What should I do if my child cannot attend a session?
Notify the school and your child's teacher via email or by calling the school.

How will I receive updates about schedules and events?
Updates and any changes will be shared via email through Mograsys by the staff leading the activity.
Will I be notified about my child's attendance?
Yes, you will receive email notifications confirming whether your child has attended or missed a session.
What is the process for raising concerns or providing feedback?
We prioritize open communication and invite parents to share any concerns or feedback through our official school email channels. Please direct your concerns or feedback to the Athletics and Activities department.
What are the expectations for parents and guardians?
Parents and guardians are kindly requested to:
• Notify the activity coordinator in advance if their child will be absent.
• Ensure timely collection of their child at the conclusion of each session (typically one hour after school dismissal).
• Prepare any necessary requirements prior to their child's participation.
What happens if activities are canceled or postponed?
Any changes or cancellations will be communicated to your registered email address as early as possible. Adjustments may occur due to operational needs or external factors.
How are student safety and well-being ensured?
AISA has implemented the following measures:
• A maximum ratio of 15 students per supervising adult
• All activity personnel are thoroughly vetted and approved as per ADEK requirements
• Staff members are trained in health and safety protocols
• On-site school nurses are available to provide support
• Upon successful allocation, parents must provide acknowledgment of consent prior to a student participating.
How does participation benefit my child?
Participation in activities supports students in developing confidence, teamwork, and a strong sense of responsibility. It also encourages positive social interaction, builds resilience, and helps students discover and grow their individual interests in a supportive environment.
